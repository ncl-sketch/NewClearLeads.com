Cold Calling Service Purchase Agreement This Cold Calling Service Purchase Agreement (the "Agreement") is made and entered into as of, by and between: Service Provider: (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider") AND Client: (hereinafter referred to as "Client") 1. Services The Service Provider agrees to provide cold calling services to the Client in accordance with the terms and conditions set forth in this Agreement. The specific details of the services, including the number of callers and target demographics, shall be mutually agreed upon by both parties. 2. Term This Agreement shall be effective from, and shall continue until, unless terminated earlier in accordance with the provisions of this Agreement. 3. Fees and Payment 4.1. Fees and Charges: The Client agrees to pay the Service Provider compensation for the services, structured based on the caller headcount and other commercial variables, as specifically set forth in the executed Order Form, which is incorporated herein by reference. This may include setup fees, recurring fees, and/or performance-based compensation. Payment shall be made by the Client to the Service Provider within days of the invoice date. Invoices will be issued on a monthly basis. 4. Responsibilities of the Parties 4.1 Service Provider Responsibilities The Service Provider shall: -Provide skilled and trained cold callers. -Maintain accurate records of calls made. -Provide weekly reports on the progress of the cold calling campaign. 4.2 Client Responsibilities The Client shall: -Provide the Service Provider with all necessary information and materials to perform the services. -Be responsible for the accuracy and legality of all information provided to the Service Provider. -Make timely payments as set forth in Section 3. 5. Confidentiality Both parties agree to keep confidential all proprietary and confidential information disclosed during the term of this Agreement. 6. Termination This Agreement may be terminated by either party with days written notice if the other party breaches any material term of this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within the notice period. 7. Governing Law This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the USA. 8. Entire Agreement This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior discussions, and agreements, whether oral or written.
